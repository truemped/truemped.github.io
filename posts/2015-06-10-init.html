About

This is my blog. I haven't really blogged ever. I so far did not think that I have something interesting to say. Given my experience at several customers and the work I am doing there I feel it might be different at least for them.

So why am I blogging then? Two things are of interest to me personally: one, I can express my opinions about different matters in a written form with more time to actually formulate my points rather than in a heated discussion. In these discussions I usually don't talk too much as I really hate being interrupted. Take aside that this is really bad behaviour during discussions I often see this and rather get me out of this as soon as possible; usually these are pointless and will not lead anywhere.

Second, I hope to make it a habit in order to get stuff out of my head.

The main topics on this blog will be information retrieval, software architecture, programming languages and agile methods. I am a professional software developer for eight years now working in the fields of information retrieval and machine learning.
